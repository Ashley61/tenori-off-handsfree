tenori-off
(it's like a Tenori-on, but shittier and in JavaScript)




What is this?

A Tenori-on is a dope electronic music instrument that Yamaha made for a hot minute. It's a fancy "sequencer", I'm told. I don't particularly like electronic music, but I love pixels and patterns and generating things out of pixels and patterns. This means I love the Tenori-on. However, I have never seen one in real life, and they're p expensive so I made a shitty JavaScript version of what I think it kind of looks like, since I don't aaaaactually know.

You can change between drums or a synth sound, export your song to a json, or send the link to a pal to have them listen to your masterpiece. It prolly has bugs so uhh, refresh when you're stuck. Ok, go make fun shit now.
